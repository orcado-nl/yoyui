# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

As an internal Orcado project, we want participation here — through code, issues, pull requests, and discussions — to be a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone involved.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the team
- Showing empathy towards other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (code, issues, pull requests, code reviews, and related discussions), and also applies when an individual is representing the project in internal Orcado channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported through Orcado's internal channels. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
